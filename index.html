Phineas Flynn Phineas Flynn is a young boy in the Flynn-Fletcher family. He is the son of Linda Flynn-Fletcher and step-son of Lawrence Fletcher. He is one of the three children of the Flynn-Fletcher household, along with his sister Candace and step-brother Ferb. He shares a strong bond with the family pet, Perry the Platypus, but remains unaware of his double life as a secret agent.
Ferb Fletcher Ferb Fletcher is a British child of the Fletcher family currently living in the American city of Danville. He is the stepson of Linda Flynn-Fletcher and the biological son of Lawrence Fletcher. Being a native of England, he speaks with an English accent. He is one of three children of the Flynn-Fletcher household along with his stepsiblings Phineas and Candace Flynn.
Isabella Garcia-Shapiro Isabella Garcia-Shapiro is the across-the-street neighbor and good friend of Phineas and Ferb. Isabella harbors feelings for Phineas, but her shyness prevents her from confessing, while he remains oblivious. She is the leader of Fireside Girls Troop 46231 and often volunteers to help in Big Ideas, usually with her troop being involved.
Baljeet Tjinder Baljeet Tjinder is a boy from India who has helped Phineas Flynn and Ferb Fletcher with their Big Ideas.
Buford Van Stomm Buford Van Stomm is a bully in the town of Danville that joins Phineas and Ferb on their adventures.
